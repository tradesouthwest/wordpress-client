TSW Sound Booster for Cell Phone.

Details

Resonator, Equalizer, Volume Enhancer

  • Made from eco-friendly materials (PLA)
  • 80mm (3.5in) W; 42mm (1.6in) D; 33mm (1.4in) H
  • Fits up to 82mm Width phones with speaker located on bottom portrait.
  • Slot to hold phone is approx. 17mm (.6in) [thickness of phone with case fit]
  • Color: Black


Features

Distributes sound across a wider surface area than the phone speaker is capable of.
Enhances volume across an equal spectrum of frequencies by grouping the speaker sound origin into a controlled vent.
Does not leave a "hollow" tunnel effect to music and voice like most phone novelties do.
Very compact size.
Can be used in Portrait or Landscape positioning.
Highs and Lows are not lost.
Easier to listen to in a working environment or in private since you can have volume lower than needed with just the phone speaker.
